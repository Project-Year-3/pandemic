Sleep Health

Learn about the manyfold benefits of good sleep and what you can do to improve your own sleep health

Learning Outcomes:

  • Understanding the benefits of sleep for mental and physical health
  • Exploring the factors that influence sleep quality
  • Learning what you can do to improve your sleep health

Teaching Methods:

  • Brainstorming
  • Quiz
  • Text

Let’s start with a little quiz about your sleep habits. This way we can tailor this session to your needs.

What causes us to sleep? The interplay of two biological processes!

Process-S:

This is your sleep drive. It increases the longer you don’t sleep and decreases once you do.

Process-C:

This is your body's natural clock, the circadian rhythm. It oscillates between night and day. Whether you actually sleep or not doesn’t matter

So when do we sleep?

When the distance between the two curves becomes too great.

Why should we sleep?

Sleep has manyfold benefits, both for your physical and mental health

Physical health benefits:

  • Strengthens the immune system
  • Reduce risk of heart disease and strokes
  • Reduce risk of cancer
  • Increases chance of successful dieting
  • Improves performance in sports and recovery
